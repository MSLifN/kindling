# Event criteria

Most hackathons publish their own judging criteria. This kit is intentionally
neutral about them — drop your event's criteria here so your team can reference
them alongside the kit's craft guidance.

Common patterns we see across hackathons:
- Business impact or real-world value
- Innovation and originality
- Technical execution
- Use of data
- Presentation and demo quality

Whatever your organizers publish, that's what matters. Use the kit's
**demo-storyteller** agent and `starter-ideas/` to shape your build around it.
