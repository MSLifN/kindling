# Judging cheatsheet

Use this while building so the team can self-score quickly.

## Award mapping

- Business Impact: Does the idea save time or solve a real problem?
- Most Innovative Idea: Is the concept surprising or fresh?
- Best Use of Data: Does the idea use data meaningfully?
- Presentation & Storytelling: Can you explain the result in under 30 seconds?
- Technical Excellence: Does the implementation feel robust and intentional?

## Build check

Before the final demo, verify:
- the user path is clear
- the output is visible
- the story is memorable
- the deployment path is documented
