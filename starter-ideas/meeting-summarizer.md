# Meeting Summarizer

## Pitch
A lightweight assistant that turns messy meeting notes into concise action items and follow-up prompts.

## Why it fits the "AI Ourselves" theme
- Most of us sit in more meetings than we have time to process afterward.
- A tool that turns "I'll write that up later" into "the action items are already in the channel" gives time back to the person running the meeting.
- The input (a transcript) and output (a few bullets) are small enough to demo live with no infrastructure.

## Demo angle
Show one meeting transcript, ask for a summary, and reveal the next steps in a few crisp bullets.
