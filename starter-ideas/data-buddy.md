# Data Buddy

## Pitch
A friendly AI companion that helps a team explore a dataset, ask follow-up questions, and turn raw data into a quick narrative.

## Why it fits the "AI Ourselves" theme
- We all spend time digging through dashboards, telemetry, and CSVs we didn't write.
- A buddy that turns "what does this column actually mean?" into a one-line answer compounds across every team that ships a product.
- Easy to wire to data you already have access to — no new pipelines required.

## Demo angle
Show one table of data, ask for a simple insight, and present the answer as a story the audience can understand.
