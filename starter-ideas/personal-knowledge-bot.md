# Personal Knowledge Bot

## Pitch
A personal AI workspace that helps you capture ideas, extract useful context, and retrieve them later in simple natural language.

## Why this works as a hackathon demo
- Every one of us has notes, snippets, and decisions scattered across OneNote, chat, email, and code review.
- A bot that captures and retrieves *your own* knowledge in plain language turns scattered context into a working memory.
- The demo is genuinely personal — you can show it remembering something you said five minutes ago.

## Demo angle
Ask the bot to remember a key idea, then retrieve it later with a question that feels natural and immediate.
