# AGENTS.md

This project is built around a POC-first mindset.

## Goal

Ship something useful, visible, and easy to explain in under 30 minutes.

## Recommended agents

1. `agents/kindling.agent.md` — orchestrates the overall theme.
2. `agents/idea-generator.agent.md` — brainstorm AI Ourselves ideas.
3. `agents/implementation-plan.agent.md` — convert idea into a tangible demo plan.
4. `agents/demo-storyteller.agent.md` — turn the build into a clear story for judging.

## Award categories to target

- Business Impact
- Most Innovative Idea
- Best Use of Data
- Presentation & Storytelling
- Technical Excellence

## POC rules

- Keep the first version small.
- Focus on one real user flow.
- Prefer a clean demo over a broad feature set.
- Make the outcome understandable in less than 30 seconds.
